About This Project

The Story

After 5+ years managing warehouse and fulfillment operations at Walmart Canada — leading teams of 45–50 associates, overseeing inbound/outbound logistics, and tracking KPIs — I realized the data behind operations was where the real decisions lived.

This project demonstrates my ability to translate operational problems into SQL queries that deliver business value.

The Database

Built a simulated warehouse & logistics schema from scratch with 8 interconnected tables: employees, customers, orders, order_items, products, inventory, warehouses, shipments, and freight_companies.

All 30 queries run against this schema and solve real problems I encountered as an Operations Manager.
